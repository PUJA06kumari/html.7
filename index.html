MANOJ KUMAR SHARMA (IPS)

In Morena, Madhya Pradesh, India, Manoj Kumar Sharma was born on Thursday, 3 July 1975 (age 49 years as of 2024) in Bilgaon Chaudhari village. A biopic titled 12th Fail about his struggles to become an IPS officer hits the headlines in October 2023. After earning his bachelor’s degree, he earned a master’s in history. His PhD is in journalism as well. The PhD was completed while he was studying for UPSC exams.

Manoj Kumar Sharma IPS Career and Education

As an IPS officer, Sharma has served in a variety of positions in Maharashtra. As Superintendent of Police in Kolhapur, he diligently handled protests regarding the tax collection on highways and sugarcane price hikes during his tenure as SP. Furthermore, he has participated in numerous counter-naxal operations in Gadchiroli, Chandrapur, Gondiya, Yavatmal, Bhandara, and Nanded. After Govind Pansare was murdered in Maharashtra in 2015, he played a vital role in defusing the agitation and preventing it from escalating. Afterward, Manoj Kumar Sharma was appointed Deputy Inspector General (DIG) by the Central Industrial Security Force (CISF). He was then posted to Mumbai as an Additional Commissioner of Police (ACP).
